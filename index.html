Biography

I am pursuing my graduate studies in Machine Learning and Data Science at University of California San Diego.

Previously, I was at Facebook Research as Software Engineer in Artificial Intelligence, from 2016-2018 where I worked on computer vision and machine learning problems.

I graduated with a B.Tech degree from Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Delhi Technological University, India in 2016.

I did summer internship in AirLab at Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh in 2015.
