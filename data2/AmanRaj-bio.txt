Aman Raj is a Deep learning research engineer at Apple, where he works on computer vision
and machine learning. He received MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering majoring in
machine learning and data science from the University of California, San Diego in 2020 and
a B.Tech in Electrical  and Communication Engineering from the Delhi Technological University
in 2012. He has received best paper award in CVPR-2017 EarthVision worskhop and
The Jack Dangermond Award - Best Paper 2018 in ISPRS Journal.
